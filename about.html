About me.

Jamie Ogundiran

AI Engineer | Researcher | Technologist

I've worked on innovative AI solutions across multiple domains, including sentiment analysis, recommender systems, and computer vision. Some of my projects include a Stock Sentiment Analyzer using Reddit data and a London Borough Recommender System.

I'm currently researching cultural bias in Large Language Models as part of my MSc in Artificial Intelligence at King’s College London. My focus is on developing more culturally adaptive LLMs for Africa, a low-resource data continent, to improve fairness and representation in AI.

Beyond research, I’ve worked in teaching and mentoring, serving as a Coding Tutor at Funtech Camp, where I introduced students to Python programming and cybersecurity concepts. This experience strengthened my ability to communicate complex ideas and develop student-centered learning experiences.

One of my proudest achievements was winning a hackathon at London Business School, where my team tackled operational inefficiencies in restaurants in developing countries. We designed an AI-driven automation system to replace manual processes, improving efficiency and scalability.
